About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a registered non-profit organization founded in 2020 and headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. Through impactful community-driven initiatives, the organization works across India to foster social empowerment, education, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, women empowerment, and community development.

Recognized by NGO Darpan (NITI Aayog) and certified under 80G, 12A, CSR-1, and ISO 9001:2015, InAmigos Foundation believes in creating sustainable change through volunteerism, collaboration, and grassroots action.

Our Mission

To foster community development and social empowerment through impactful and sustainable initiatives that uplift lives and create equal opportunities.

Our Vision

To build an equitable and inclusive society where every individual has access to education, support, dignity, and opportunities for growth.

Our Projects

Project SEVA

Distributes meals, clothing, and essential supplies to underprivileged and homeless communities.

Impact: Reduces hunger and supports vulnerable populations.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Provides child education, digital literacy, academic support, and life-skills training.

Impact: Empowers children through education and future opportunities.

Project JEEV

Dedicated to animal welfare through feeding, rescue support, shelter assistance, and compassionate care.

Impact: Improves the well-being of stray and vulnerable animals.

Project PRAKRITI

Promotes environmental sustainability through tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and awareness programs.

Impact: Encourages greener communities and environmental responsibility.

Project UDAAN

Focuses on women empowerment through skill development, awareness initiatives, and leadership opportunities.

Impact: Helps women become socially and economically independent.

Project VIKAS

Provides skill-building programs, internships, webinars, and career guidance for students and young professionals.

Impact: Enhances employability and professional development.
